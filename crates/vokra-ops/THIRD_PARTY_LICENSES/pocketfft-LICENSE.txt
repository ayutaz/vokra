Vokra's CPU FFT lowering (crates/vokra-ops/src/fft/*) is a from-scratch Rust
reimplementation of the algorithm of pocketfft by Martin Reinecke. pocketfft is
distributed under the following 3-clause BSD license, reproduced here to satisfy
its source-redistribution conditions (retain the copyright notice, the list of
conditions, and the disclaimer).

Upstream: https://gitlab.mpcdf.mpg.de/mtr/pocketfft (C reference implementation)
